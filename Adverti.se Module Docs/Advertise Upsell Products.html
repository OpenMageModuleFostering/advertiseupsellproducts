Activating Adverti.se Upsell Products

Thank you for installing our Upsell Products module.

Before upsell products will appear in the front end of your site you need to complete the following simple modifications to your current theme's template files. Once you have completed the steps below you will see our upsell product suggestions appear on your product pages:

Step 1

Find the template file upsell.phtml used by your theme. You should find it here:

<Magento-root-folder>/app/design/frontend/<theme-package>/<theme-name>/template/catalog/product/list/upsell.phtml

Copy and paste these lines at the top of the file: 

  
  <div id="advupsell">
  <script type="text/javascript">
    if(adv_upsell_reload){
      loadAdvertiseUpsellProducts();
      adv_upsell_reload=false;
    }
  </script>

And add this line at the end of the file:

  
  </div>

Step 2

Find the template file head.phtml used by your theme. You should find it here:

<Magento-root-folder>/app/design/frontend/<theme-package>/<theme-name>/template/page/html/head.phtml

Copy and paste this script tag somewhere within the <head> element in that file: 

  
    <script type="text/javascript">
      <?php 
        echo Mage::helper('advertise_retailintelligence')->getAdvertiseHeaderScript();
      ?>
    </script>

Step 3

If you have not done so already, register your Adverti.se account by saving your details in the Magento Admin panel at:

System → Configuration → Adverti.se → Account Settings

At the same time your product data will be sent to us for processing, to enable us to suggest relevant products from your store.
